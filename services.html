Accounting Process Review and Transformation.

Our Accounting Process review and transformation is a very detailed approach involving the following procedures:

  • Review of existing Accounting and Financial system
  • Assess internal controls and key performance measures
  • Assess key accounting processes and alignment with industry best practices
  • Develop processes and procedures of key accounting functions
  • Design/Review Chart of Accounts
  • Identify potential technology-related solutions
  • Data migration (Where necessary)

Finance function resourcing and outsourcing.

We offer a full range of accounting services that cut across your day-to-day operations and ensuring compliance with relevant accounting, tax, and regulatory requirements at all stages in your business life cycle. These functions could be wholly outsourced to us or you could require us to second human resources to meet your needs. We offer both full finance outsourcing and secondment services. Whichever works for your business model.

Audit and Assurance Service.

Our independent audit service is carried out without compromising the principle of independence and is done in compliance with the International Financial Reporting Standards. With our risk-oriented approach, we offer you more than merely auditing the correctness of your accounts, we offer a comprehensive review of your systems and controls as part of the Audit engagement.
